Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about InsightAlly

How do I join InsightAlly?

Simply click the "Sign Up" button and create your account. You'll need to provide your student or alumni information to verify your eligibility.

Is InsightAlly free to use?

Yes! InsightAlly is completely free for students and alumni. We believe in making networking and mentorship accessible to everyone.

How can I connect with alumni?

You can browse our alumni directory, join networking events, or participate in mentorship programs to connect with experienced professionals.

Can I post job opportunities?

Absolutely! Alumni can post job opportunities and internships for current students. This helps create a bridge between education and employment.

How do I report inappropriate content?

If you encounter any inappropriate content, please contact our support team immediately. We take community safety seriously.

Can I organize events?

Yes! Both students and alumni can propose and organize events. We encourage community-driven initiatives and collaboration.
